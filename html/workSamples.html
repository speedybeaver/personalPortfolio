Work Samples

Math Presentation

In my GAT and IDS classes at MMSTC, we were tasked with making a presentation to teach middle schoolers at the MS2TC program about middle school math concepts. The purpose of this was to prove our understand of these simple concepts, and to teach middle schoolers about something they may not know in our own creative way.

Bridge Presentation

In my IDS class, we participated in the MDOT bridge competition, and as per a requirement to enter the competition, we made a presentation to introduce ourselves and our bridge design, as well as the principles and science behind our design.

Focus Question: Odyssey

In my Honors English 9 class, we regularly read fictional and nonfictional novels, and every few chapters, we're tasked with giving our opinion on a certain part of the book through what our teacher calls a "Focus Question".

Lab Report

In my 9th grade Biology class at MMSTC, we have done several experiments that each take a few months to complete. At the end of each experiment, or lab, we make a lab report to sum up our findings.

Biome Project

In my 9th grade Biology class at MMSTC, we were assigned a biome and were tasked with making a presentation about the flora, fauna, resources, and other features.
